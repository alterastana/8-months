To My Dearest Love

"To the One Who Deserved Better..."

As we mark eight months together, my heart is filled with both love and deep regret. I've come to realize how my actions have hurt you, and this realization weighs heavily on me every day. You gave me your trust, your love, and your kindness, and in return, I brought you pain.

I want you to know how truly sorry I am for all the hurt I've caused. For the times I was careless with your feelings, for the moments I failed to be the partner you deserved, and for the wounds I inflicted on your heart. Looking back, I can see clearly how my behavior darkened what should have been our brightest days.

This anniversary is not just a celebration of our time together, but also my promise to change. I am committed to becoming better - to listening more, to being more thoughtful, to putting your happiness first. I know words alone can't undo the past, but I hope my actions moving forward will show you the depth of my remorse and my dedication to our love.

You are the most precious person in my life, and the thought of having caused you pain is unbearable. I vow to spend every day making amends, to be the safe harbor you deserve rather than the storm that disrupted your peace. My love for you is eternal, and so is my commitment to earn back your trust.

With all my heart, I ask for your forgiveness and the chance to prove myself worthy of your love again.

Happy Eight Months, My Love
